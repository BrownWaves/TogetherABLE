About this Voice

Maria Adara Bukchinella M. Carreon is a student feature and medical enthusiast. In her free time, she likes to learn about Deaf culture, medicine, health care systems, and the nuances of disabled life in different countries. She believes that everyone has value and that there are many potential history makers hidden by societal prejudice. Thus, she hopes to be an ally in bridging the gap between people of different abilities.
